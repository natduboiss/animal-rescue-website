Welcome to Freedom Dog Rescue

Making freedom happen one dog at a time.

Who we are

Freedom Dog Rescue is a non-profit registered charity, which was founded in Ottawa in February 2015. We are an all-based, all-size foster based rescue which operates in Ottawa and surrounding areas.

Look at our dogs

What we're about

We are dedicated to rescuing homeless and abandoned dogs, dogs from shelters and taking in desperate owner surrenders when they can no longer care for them.
